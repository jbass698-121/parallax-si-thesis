Why the Multiple Expands

Three structural shifts plus a live PE-backed comp

SHIFT 1

Recurring revenue

A 50%+ recurring services business commands a fundamentally different risk profile. Strategics and PE pay 10–13x for recurring-heavy services; 5–7x for project-only.

SHIFT 2

AI-enabled delivery as proprietary IP

Not a tool, an asset. Defensible delivery cost advantages, faster time-to-value, scalable engine. Autonomous Systems and AI-Driven Automation subsectors saw 3–4x multiple expansion 2023–2025.

SHIFT 3

Platform optionality

A 3-year-old, $28M-revenue, AI-native AVEVA delivery platform with 55%+ recurring revenue is attractive for further roll-up — PE platform OR strategic tuck-in.

Honest framing on the exit multiple

A $9.5M EBITDA business is at the small end of what most PE platforms underwrite at 10–12x in standalone form — true platform multiples typically unlock at $15M+ EBITDA. The 10–12x exit range therefore assumes either (a) a Year-2 or Year-3 bolt-on lifts the business above $15M EBITDA, or (b) a strategic acquirer (such as the InflexionPoint / Sverica platform discussed below) pays a premium for geographic and vertical fit. Base case where neither happens: 9–11x exit, 7.5x–9.5x MOIC — still a strong PE return.

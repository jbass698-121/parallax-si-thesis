MW/MVA? Any significant motor loads, drives, or non-linear loads? Harmonics issues?

Current Tools & Models

Pain & Challenges
Step 3 of 6 · Surface business pain and operational risk — this drives the demo
⚡ MEDDPICC — Implicate the Pain
Your goal: Identify → Indicate → Implicate. Pain without implication = no budget. Drive them to feel the cost of inaction.
Safety & Arc Flash
ETAP's highest-impact use case for most sites
10When did you last complete an arc flash hazard analysis? Was it NFPA 70E / IEEE 1584-2018 compliant?
Outdated arc flash = massive liability. Post-2018 IEEE 1584 often changes results significantly.
11Have there been any electrical incidents, near-misses, or OSHA recordables in the past 3 years?
High sensitivity — treat carefully. Any "yes" = urgent business case.
Operational Reliability
Downtime, protection, and reliability pain points
12How often do you experience unplanned electrical outages or nuisance trips? What's the cost per hour of downtime?
Force them to quantify. $10K/hr vs. $1M/hr changes the ROI conversation completely.
13Are your protective relays coordinated? When was the last protection coordination study?
Miscoordinated protection = selective fault isolation failure = wider outages. Classic ETAP SC/coordination value.
14Are there plans for system expansion, load additions, or major capital projects in the next 2 years?
Growth = new studies required. Brownfield + expansion = ETAP digital twin value is immediate.
Power Quality & Renewables
Emerging issues that ETAP directly addresses
15Do you have power quality issues — harmonics, voltage sags, transients, motor starting problems?
PQ issues = ETAP Harmonic Analysis and Transient Stability modules. Often under-recognized pain.
Operations & Compliance
Step 4 of 6 · Understand their operational workflow, team, and regulatory context
Team & Workflow
Who does studies, how they work, and what slows them down
16Who performs power system studies in your organization? Internal team or outsourced to consultants?
Internal team = ETAP as productivity tool. Outsourced = "can you bring it in-house?" value conversation.
17How long does it typically take to complete a power study today? What takes the most time?
Model building / data gathering is usually 60-70% of effort. ETAP automated parameterization and libraries cut this dramatically.
18How do you manage and track changes to the power system? Is there a formal change management process?
Ad-hoc change management = model drift, outdated labels, wrong PPE ratings. Big risk story.
Regulatory & Compliance
Compliance requirements that drive urgency and budget
19What regulatory or insurance requirements drive your electrical safety program?
OSHA, NFPA 70E, NEC, IEC 61000, NERC CIP, insurance underwriters, customer audits?
20Do you need to produce formal study reports for management, insurance, or regulators?
Automated report generation in ETAP = massive time saver. Great demo moment.

Buying Process & Budget
Step 6 of 6 · MEDDPICC — Economic Buyer, Decision Process, and Champion
🔑 MEDDPICC Qualification
This section completes your MEDDPICC qualification. Without understanding the buying process, even the best demo won't close a deal.
Economic Buyer & Champion
Find the money and the internal advocate
26Who has budget authority for this type of investment? Is that person in the room today?
If not in the room, you need a Champion who can reach them. Don't leave without a path to the Economic Buyer.
27Is there budget allocated for this? What is the approximate budget range?
Don't shy from the question. Frame it: "To make sure I bring you the right solution, what investment range is realistic for this project?"
Decision Process & Timeline
Map the path from demo to signed order
28What does your evaluation and decision process look like? Who else needs to be involved?
IT? Legal? Procurement? Safety committee? How many approvals? What could stop this?
29What would a successful outcome look like in 12 months after deploying ETAP?
This is gold for the demo. Their answer = your demo success criteria. Write it down verbatim.
30What would prevent this from moving forward? What are the biggest risks to this project?
Surface objections now so you can address them in the demo. Budget frozen? IT security? Change resistance?
